About

Building systems that let language scale without losing soul.

I'm Abby — a localization leader with over 9 years shaping multilingual content for global digital health companies. I build scalable localization systems, lead cross-functional teams, and develop strategies that harness AI to deliver high-quality content efficiently across markets.

My background sits at the intersection of mental health content strategy and localization strategy — which means I understand both the sensitivity required in wellness communication and the operational rigour needed to deliver it at scale.

I'm particularly drawn to the intersection of human expertise and AI innovation: designing pre-translation workflows, building cultural nuance frameworks, and helping teams use AI tools in ways that genuinely improve quality — not just speed.

A particular focus on health & wellness platforms.

Content in mental health and digital wellbeing isn't interchangeable across markets. What resonates in one culture may land badly — or cause harm — in another. I bring both the localization expertise and the domain sensitivity to navigate that.

Whether you're a startup planning your first international expansion or an established platform auditing your existing localization operations, I can help you understand what the work actually requires, what's realistic, and how to do it well.
